2. Random Number Gallery - An Idea Generator & Art Gallery Database Website

    Link:
    Struggling with writer's block?
    Can't seem to think up a new idea for something to draw?
    Try Random-Generated-Gallery!

    Technologies & Skills Used:

      Javascript, HTML, CSS, Node.js, Multer, Argon2, Github, Procedural Generation.

    Key Features:

      1. Idea Generator
        Users simply click the Generate Idea button and it creates a drawing idea concept from a series of keywords stored within the database with a variety of sentence structures, instantly crushing writer's block. The Idea Generator is general purpose and may be used for any creative field as well.

      2. User Idea Keyword Uploading
        Users are able to add new keywords to the database, enriching the generator's capabilities in much the same way that wikipedia grows exponentially due to the efforts of users themselves.

      3. User Art Upload
        Once you generate an idea, draw it! When you're done, be sure to upload it to RNG.

      4. Shareable User Galleries with Pagination
        Once uploaded, your artwork can be found in your personal gallery that you may share with friends whenever you want.

        Galleries use pagination to deal with potentially large gallery sizes.

        I lead a group of four software engineers from various backgrounds in developing a web application known as DnDone.

        I was responsible for building the map generator, designing the graphic design of the application, delegating tasks, keeping the project on schedule, assisting other team members in their modules, and leading the presentation of our product.

      2. Procedural Content Generation
